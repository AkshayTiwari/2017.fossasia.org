Topics

Technology and Startups

Join us on in March 2017 and learn about new developments in software and hardware. Meet developers of Open Source knitting machines, Laptops that can send and receive on all frequencies, web technologies like Javascript, PHP, Ruby, HTML, Git, Android, DevOps, Docker and much more.

OpenTech and Science Hacks

The FOSSASIA weekend in March 2017 is dedicated to the Internet of Things. What are the technologies and scientific achievements that make our world run? We have tracks on Open Source software and customized hardware, Arduinos, Rapsberry PI, Open Design applications and the maker and hacker community. In the Science Hack tracks you will learn about how you can participate in the Citizen Science community.

A little more about FOSSASIA

FOSSASIA is the premier Free and Open Source technology event in Asia for developers, start-ups, and contributors. Projects at FOSSASIA range from open hardware, to design, graphics and software. FOSSASIA was established in 2009. Previous events took place in Cambodia and Vietnam.

FOSSASIA is set over three days of ground-breaking talks and workshops for OpenTech enthusiasts

  • DAY 1 Business Day

    Open technologies in the industry and startups

  • DAY 2 Full Monty Open Tech in 15 tracks with Talks and Workshops

    OpenTech in all varieties and from all angels: Software, Hardware, Policies, Design Hacking, Virtual Reality, Biohacking and Science

  • DAY 3 Full Monty Open Tech continues with Talks and Workshops

    OpenTech in all varieties and from all angels: Software, Hardware, Policies, Design Hacking, Virtual Reality, Biohacking and Science.

FOSSASIA is the annual OpenTechSummit featuring prominent tech icons from around the world.
